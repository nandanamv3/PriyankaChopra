ABOUT

Priyanka Chopra Jonas is an Indian actress and producer. The winner of the Miss World 2000 pageant, Chopra is one of India's highest-paid actresses and has received numerous accolades, including two National Film Awards and five Filmfare Awards. In 2016, the Government of India honoured her with the Padma Shri, and Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world. In the next two years, Forbes listed her among the World's 100 Most Powerful Women, and in 2022, she was named in the BBC 100 Women list.

CAREER

  1. Acting Career:
    Bollywood: Priyanka began her acting career in Bollywood and quickly rose to stardom with notable films like "Andaaz," "Aitraaz," "Don," and "Fashion." She has received numerous awards for her performances, including the prestigious National Film Award for her role in "Fashion." Hollywood: Priyanka made a successful transition to Hollywood with her role as Alex Parrish in the popular TV series "Quantico." She has also starred in several Hollywood films, including "Baywatch" and "Isn't It Romantic." Her work has received critical acclaim and expanded her global fanbase.


  2. Music Career:
    Priyanka is not only a talented actress but also a versatile singer. She released her debut single "In My City" in 2012, followed by "Exotic" featuring Pitbull. Her music career has further demonstrated her artistic prowess and cultural diversity.

  3. Producing and Philanthropy:
    Purple Pebble Pictures: Priyanka co-founded Purple Pebble Pictures, a production company aimed at promoting regional Indian cinema. The company has produced several successful films and web series, helping showcase diverse storytelling. UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador: Priyanka serves as a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador and actively supports various charitable causes, particularly those related to children's rights and education. She is an advocate for girls' empowerment and has worked towards improving healthcare and education for underprivileged children.

  4. Author:
    Priyanka is also an accomplished author. Her memoir, "Unfinished," released in 2021, offers an intimate look into her life, career, and personal journey. It received widespread acclaim for its candid storytelling.

  5. Entrepreneurship:
    Priyanka has ventured into entrepreneurship with her skincare brand, "Anomaly," and her restaurant in New York City, "Sona." These ventures reflect her commitment to promoting diversity and inclusivity.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Chopra has won a National Film Award for Best Actress for Fashion (2008) and five Filmfare Awards: Best Female Debut for Andaaz (2003), Best Performance in a Negative Role for Aitraaz (2004), Best Actress for Fashion (2008), Critics Award for Best Actress for 7 Khoon Maaf (2011), and Best Supporting Actress for Bajirao Mastani (2015). She has also won two People's Choice Awards: "Favourite Actress In A New TV Series", and "Favorite Dramatic TV Actress" for Quantico. She is the first South Asian actress to win a People's Choice Award. In 2016, she was awarded the Padma Shri, the fourth highest civilian award, by the Government of India for her contribution to arts and was honored as one of the BBC 100 Women in 2022.
